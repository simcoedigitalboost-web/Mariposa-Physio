Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation services tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Physiotherapy & Manual Therapy

Physiotherapy is a holistic approach to the prevention, diagnosis, and therapeutic management of pain, movement disorders, and the optimization of physical function. At Mariposa, our therapists have completed post-graduate studies to develop advanced manual therapy skills through the Orthopaedic Division of the Ontario Physiotherapy Association.

Our treatment approach utilizes education, manual therapy techniques, exercise prescription, and modalities as required to assist in your recovery. We focus on attaining proper joint mechanics while using soft tissue techniques in combination with exercise and posture correction for lasting results.

Learn More About Physiotherapy

Pelvic Health Physiotherapy

Pelvic health physiotherapy is a specialized area focusing on the rehabilitation of muscles in the pelvic floor after injury or dysfunction. Our therapists are trained to assess and treat conditions affecting both women and men, including urinary incontinence, pelvic pain, prenatal and postnatal care, and pelvic organ prolapse.

Learn More About Pelvic Health

Lymphedema & Cancer Rehabilitation

Lymphedema is swelling that occurs in any body area, typically the extremities, as a result of trauma or damage to the lymphatic vessel system. This commonly occurs after surgery or radiation where lymph nodes have been directly affected.

Our certified Combined Decongestive Therapy (CDT) therapist can help you manage your lymphedema and teach you to effectively self-manage the condition. Kelly Spears is a registered Authorizer for the Government Assistive Devices Program (ADP) for lymphedema garments.

Learn More About Lymphedema Treatment

Concussion & Vestibular Rehabilitation

Vestibular rehabilitation is a specialized form of physiotherapy intended to treat and improve symptoms caused by vestibular (inner ear) disorders. People with vestibular disorders often experience dizziness, vertigo, visual disturbances, motion sensitivity, and balance problems.

We also provide comprehensive concussion assessment and rehabilitation, addressing the multiple systems that can be affected including the vestibular system, cervical spine, and exertion tolerance.

Learn More About Concussion & Vestibular Rehab

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the insertion of thin sterile needles through the skin into soft tissues. Our physiotherapists who incorporate acupuncture have completed post-graduate clinical education through institutions recognized by the College of Physiotherapists of Ontario and are certified through Acupuncture Canada.

Acupuncture can be used alongside other physiotherapy treatments to help with pain management, muscle tension, and recovery.

Learn More About Acupuncture

Massage Therapy

Our Registered Massage Therapist works collaboratively with the physiotherapy team to assist in your care. Open communication ensures your goals are met without duplication of services.

Massage therapy may be utilized to help with the reduction of pain, muscular tension, improved range of motion, and decreased tissue adhesions. It can also be useful for stress reduction, which often causes pain syndromes in the back or neck, headaches, and muscle tension.

Learn More About Massage Therapy
