Our Team

Dedicated professionals committed to helping you achieve your health and rehabilitation goals.

Kelly Spears

Owner & Registered Physiotherapist

BPhEd, BHScPT, MClScPT (manip.), CAFCI, CDT, MCPA, FCAMPT

Kelly has over 25 years of clinical experience in private practice physiotherapy. She graduated with Honours from Brock University in 1998 with her Physical Education Degree, then attended McMaster University and graduated with Distinction in 2000 with her Bachelor of Health Science in Physiotherapy.

After seven years of practice and numerous continuing education courses including the McKenzie method and certification in Acupuncture, she attended the University of Western Ontario for her Master in Clinical Science Physiotherapy Degree. She then passed the Orthopaedic Advanced exams, becoming a Fellow of the Canadian Academy of Manual and Manipulative Physiotherapy (FCAMPT).

Kelly furthered her education and became certified to treat Lymphedema using Combined Decongestive Therapy (CDT). She is a registered Authorizer for the Government Assistive Devices Program (ADP) for lymphedema garments. She is also trained in Pelvic Health Physiotherapy for the treatment of pelvic pain syndromes and urinary incontinence.

Her treatment philosophy involves using manual therapy techniques to attain proper joint mechanics, combined with soft tissue techniques, exercise, and posture correction to achieve muscular balance and preventative maintenance.

Wendi Kohler

Registered Physiotherapist

HBAKin, MPT, DPT

After graduating with an Honours Bachelor degree in Kinesiology and working as a Kinesiologist in Toronto, Wendi pursued Physiotherapy as a career. She graduated Summa Cum Laude from Saint Louis University in 2006 with a Master's of Physical Therapy degree and continued to complete her Doctor of Physical Therapy degree in 2008.

Wendi practiced in both inpatient and private outpatient settings in Missouri and North Dakota before returning to Canada. She began her Canadian career at Royal Victoria Regional Health Centre in Barrie, working in a variety of settings over her 12 years with RVH including outpatient, post-surgical, rehabilitation, cardiac, and critical cardiac care.

She has mentored many physiotherapy and physiotherapy assistant students and has been an assessor for the Physiotherapy Competency Exam. Wendi focuses primarily on orthopaedic patients, is BoneFit Certified, and is a facilitator for the GLA:D (Good Life with Arthritis) program.

Wendi enjoys supporting patients in achieving optimal health, which requires addressing both the body and mind. She takes a holistic approach to her own life and practices yoga and breathwork regularly as part of her healthy lifestyle.

Marie Picard

Registered Massage Therapist

RMT

Marie graduated from the Canadian College of Massage and Hydrotherapy in 2016 and started her practice that same year. With a special interest in proper functional movement and posture, Marie helps clients manage both chronic and acute injuries.

Encouraging the involvement of her clients in their own healing through education and support is the norm. Be prepared for some homework to accelerate your recovery process.

Marie has acquired additional certifications to enhance her knowledge and skills, including Cupping, Dynamic Taping, and Advanced Neurological Techniques. She has also completed a certification program regarding Autism Spectrum Disorder, offering interactive custom treatments for neurological symptom management.

When away from the clinic, Marie is an autism advocate, meeting with researchers and members of the government regularly. In her free time, she enjoys time with her family, trekking through the forest, foraging, gardening, and taking in the sounds of the great outdoors.
